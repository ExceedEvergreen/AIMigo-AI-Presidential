Project description

AIMigo helps teens reflect on their mood in under 60 seconds using voice, while minimizing what gets stored. It turns a short voice journal into an interpretable emotional summary and trend charts over time, emphasizing patterns rather than one off judgments. AIMigo is explicitly non clinical: it does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional mental health care, and it is designed to support reflection with privacy first defaults and safety guardrails.
